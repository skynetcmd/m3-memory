Frequently asked questions

Q: What is the warranty period?
A: All widgets carry a 2-year limited warranty from the date of
purchase. Extended warranty is available for an additional fee.

Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping takes
1-2 business days. International shipping varies by destination but
typically 10-14 business days.

Q: Do you offer bulk discounts?
A: Yes. Orders of 50 or more units receive a 10 percent discount.
Orders of 200 or more receive a 18 percent discount. Contact sales for
volume above 1000 units.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, wire transfer, and
purchase orders for verified business customers.
