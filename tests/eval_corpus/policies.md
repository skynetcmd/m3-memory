# Company policies

## Vacation

Full-time employees accrue 20 days of vacation per calendar year.
Part-time employees accrue 12 days. Unused vacation does not carry
over past December 31st.

## Remote work

Engineers may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Sales staff and
finance roles must be on-site at least 4 days per week. Onboarding
requires the first 6 weeks in-office for all roles.

## Equipment

Each new hire receives a laptop, a 27-inch monitor, and a $500 home
office stipend. Replacement cycles are 3 years for laptops and 5 years
for monitors.
