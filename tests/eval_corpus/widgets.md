# Widget reference guide

Widgets ship in three sizes: small, medium, and large.

## Small widgets

Small widgets weigh 1.2 kilograms each. They come in red, blue, and
green. Maximum operating temperature is 45 degrees Celsius. The
recommended duty cycle is 50 percent.

## Medium widgets

Medium widgets weigh 4.7 kilograms each. They are available in black
and silver. Maximum operating temperature is 60 degrees Celsius. The
recommended duty cycle is 70 percent.

## Large widgets

Large widgets weigh 12.5 kilograms each. They come only in
graphite. Maximum operating temperature is 85 degrees Celsius. The
recommended duty cycle is 100 percent — they can run continuously.

## Compatibility

All widgets use the standard MX-9 mounting bracket. Cable length is 2.5
meters by default. Custom lengths up to 8 meters are available on
request.
